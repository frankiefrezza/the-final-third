Release Notes
Version Update 1.0.7
Premium Light Mode
Latest
The "Parchment & Espresso" Theme
You can now toggle between our classic "Navy Slate & Walnut" dark mode and our new "Parchment & Espresso" light mode. Perfect for reading tasting notes in bright sunlight. Access the toggle in your Profile settings.
Version Update 1.0.6
Native Gestures & Reviews
Swipe to Action Engine
We have completely removed the clunky edit buttons. You can now intuitively swipe left or drag any entry in your Humidor or Journal to seamlessly reveal the Edit and Remove tools across both touchscreens and computer mice.
Dynamic External Reviews
Tapping the Daily Selection at the top of your screen will now bring up a custom prompt, routing you directly to industry reviews and ratings.
Version Update 1.0.5
Cloud Sync & Advanced Tracking
Cloud Sync
Your profile and journal are now securely stored in the cloud. Simply log in from any device to access your data wherever you are.
Daily Cigar Suggestion
A new featured cigar is displayed at the top of the home screen each day. With a library of 365 cigars, you'll receive a different recommendation every day throughout the year.
Quantity Tracking
A new Quantity field has been added, allowing you to keep track of how many cigars you have for each entry in your humidor.
Improved Aging Tracking
Added Date Acquired and Peak Aging Date fields when adding a cigar. The Peak Aging Date requires a minimum of seven days after the acquisition date to promote realistic aging timelines.
Photo Attachments
You can now attach photos to Humidor and Journal entries. Entries containing photos will display an image indicator, making them easy to identify while browsing your collection.
Enhanced Profile Statistics
The Profile page now automatically generates statistics based on your Humidor and Journal entries, including Average Humidor Cost, Average Aging Time, Favorite Cut, and Favorite Light.
