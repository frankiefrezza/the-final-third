Version Update
Latest Features & Improvements

This update includes several new features and improvements designed to enhance your experience with The Final Third.

Cloud Sync
Your profile and journal are now securely stored in the cloud. Simply log in from any device to access your data wherever you are.
Daily Cigar Suggestion
A new featured cigar is displayed at the top of the home screen each day. With a library of 365 cigars, you'll receive a different recommendation every day throughout the year.
Custom Nickname
You can now edit your nickname directly from the Welcome screen, making it easier to personalize your profile.
Quantity Tracking
A new Quantity field has been added, allowing you to keep track of how many cigars you have for each entry in your humidor.
Improved Aging Tracking
Added Date Acquired and Peak Aging Date fields when adding a cigar. The Peak Aging Date requires a minimum of seven days after the acquisition date to promote realistic aging timelines.
Photo Attachments
You can now attach photos to Humidor and Journal entries. Entries containing photos will display an image indicator, making them easy to identify while browsing your collection.
Edit Entries
An Edit button has been added below the Remove button in both the Humidor and Journal sections. You can now update existing entries without having to delete and recreate them.
Enhanced Profile Statistics
The Profile page now automatically generates statistics based on your Humidor and Journal entries, including:
  • Average Humidor Cost
  • Average Aging Time
  • Favorite Cut
  • Favorite Lighting Method
These statistics update automatically as you continue to build your collection and journal your experiences.
