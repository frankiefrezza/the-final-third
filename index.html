Release Notes
Version Update 1.1.1
Log a Smoke UI Revamp
Latest
Searchable Modal
The "Log a Smoke" action now opens a clean pop-up modal overlay instead of navigating you to the humidor. You can search directly within the modal to easily find and deplete your inventory before writing your journal entry.
Version Update 1.1.0
Streamlined Logging Workflow
Separated Actions
Humidor cards no longer act as click targets until you explicitly activate the "Log a Smoke" feature. This completely prevents accidental removal prompts while browsing your inventory.
Photo Badge Isolation
Fixed an event bubbling bug where tapping "View Photo" on a card would incorrectly trigger humidor actions instead of just opening the image.
Version Update 1.0.9
Search, Sort & Collapsible Entries
Search & Filter
Added a dedicated search bar and sorting toggle (A-Z vs Date) just beneath the Journal and Aging Timeline headers to easily parse your collection.
Collapsible Journal Cards
Journal history entries are now collapsed by default to save vertical screen space. Tap the chevron icon on any card to reveal your detailed tasting notes and smoking mechanics.
